Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Mood Frame Coach is a photo composition and editing preview app. This policy explains what data the current app uses and how it is handled.

Photos and camera access

  • Camera access is requested only after you tap a camera action.
  • Photo library access is requested only after you choose a photo action.
  • The current release uses selected or captured photos for local preview only.
  • The current release does not upload photos to a server.
  • Selected or captured photos are not shared with RevenueCat, AdMob, analytics providers, or any other third-party service in the current release.
  • Photo data is session-only and is discarded when you leave the flow, choose another photo, or close the app.

Accounts, contacts, and location

  • No account is required.
  • Contacts are not uploaded or accessed.
  • Location is not requested.

Analytics, ads, and purchases

  • The current release uses a local event counter only and does not include an external analytics SDK.
  • Rewarded ads are available only when an approved ad network is configured.
  • Pro purchase screens show the available App Store plans when products are configured for the release.

Future services

If cloud AI processing, external analytics, RevenueCat, or ad networks are enabled in a future release, this policy will be updated before submission.
